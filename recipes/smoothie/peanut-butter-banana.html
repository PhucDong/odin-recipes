Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

This peanut butter banana smoothie is so refreshing, and it's sweet and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Almond butter and almond milk
  • 1 scoop of chocolate whey protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon of chocolate syrup
  • 1 cup of vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup of plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 splash of vanilla extract and caramel syrup

Steps

  1. Freeze the bananas first overnight.
  2. Add 1 cup whole milk, banana, peanut butter, cinnamon, and ice to a blender carafe. Start the blender on low and gradually increase to high.
  3. Stop and scrape down the sides of the blender as needed. If the smoothie is too thick add additional milk, one tablespoon at a time, to reach desired consistency.
  4. Blend until the smoothie is frothy and smooth with ice completely blended. Serve.
